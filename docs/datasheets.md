# Chip datasheets

Here are some datasheets of the chips used in the MailStation:

* [29F080A](../datasheets/29F080A.pdf) - the Flash chip containing the operating system (codeflash - 1 MB).
* [28SF040](../datasheets/28SF040.pdf) - the Flash chip containing the user data (dataflash - 512 kB).
* [HY628100B](../datasheets/HY628100B.PDF) - the RAM chip (128 kB)
* [RCV336DPFSP](../datasheets/RCV336DPFSP.pdf) - the modem chip used in the DET1 machines.
* [CX88168](../datasheets/CX88168.pdf) - the modem chip used in the DET2 machines.
* [RC224ATL](../datasheets/RC224ATL.pdf) - the modem chip used in the DET1D/DET1E machines.
* [TC8521](../datasheets/TC8521.pdf) - the real time clock chip, whose function is emulated by the I/O ports `0x10` through `0x1F`.
